✒️ About Me

My name is Kerk Day, and I am a web developer and freelance designer. I recently graduated from a two year degree in Software Development at SAIT Polytechnic, and am currently looking for work.

Email me here if you’re looking to hire!

So far, I’ve had the opportunity to create designs and websites for a few local businesses and personalities. Some of my early public work includes some poster designs and promotional materials for Trapped Calgary, and a logo for Club MR.

More recently, I’ve been working with The Fellowship of Alberta Bears on replacing their old and outdated website, and with some friends to make a cover for The World at Night Podcast. I’ve also spent my quarantine time learning about the different technologies I’ve seen posted on job offerings.

Most notable things I’ve practiced with are Vue.js and React.js for front end development; Node.js with Express.js, Golang, and PHP; as well as working with remote servers like Amazon Web Services(AWS) and Vultr.
I chose Vultr because I had a lot of free credit for their services, and their service is really simple to operate. They’re quite similar to Digital Ocean in that regard.

I am constantly working to better myself and to make a dent in this technological landscape. Thank you for looking at my website, I hope you found it entertaining.
